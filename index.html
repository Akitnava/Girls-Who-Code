WaterPolo

Are you looking to play a team sport, but can't find one that you are particularly good at? Are you a comfortable swimmer, but don't enjoy competetive swimming too much? If so, water polo might be the sport for you. It helps build muscle, and burns many calories while playing it. It teaches you teamwork and cooperation with others.
